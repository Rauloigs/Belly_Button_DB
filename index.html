Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

The volunteers for Roza's project should be able to identify the top 10 bacterial species in their belly buttons in this Dashboard. That way, if Improbable Beef identifies a species as a candidate to manufacture synthetic beef, Roza's volunteers will be able to identify whether that species is found in their navel. In the following table you will be able to have the explanation to understand each visualization of this Dashboard:

# Information Description Viz Type
1 Demographic Info Your general information List Type
2 Top 10 Bacterias In case you have more than 10 Bar Chart
3 Washing Frequency Your weekly scrubs Gauge Chart
4 Cultures per Sample How many of those bacterias Bubble Chart
